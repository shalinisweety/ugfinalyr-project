Top 5 Best Trekking Places in Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is blessed with abundant beauty. Guarded by the Himalayas, the world’s youngest and tallest mountain range, Himachal Pradesh is also home to a rich culture and varied flora and fauna. Blessed with three mountain ranges, Dhauladhar, Pir Panjal, and the Himalayas, it is bound to be the adventure capital of the country. Understandably, trekking tops the list of adventure activities that are conducted over here. Himachal Pradesh can rightly be termed as the epitome of beauty with its mixed forest of pine, deodar, birch, silver oak, and many more varieties. Not only that, but the presence of many major rivers like the Beas, Chenab, Ravi, Satluj, and Yamuna enhance the beauty of this state. Trekking in such a place is an irresistible thing. Though all the treks in Himachal Pradesh are very beautiful, there are a few favorites that we would like to share with you.

Hampta Pass

Hampta Pass is famous for its beautiful landscape and different terrains that never disappoint. Witness the postcard-type landscapes, have the bone-chilling experience of a stream crossing, get an afternoon nap alongside the beautiful wildflowers and find solitude at Chandratal Lake. Hampta Pass introduces you to the technique of stream crossing, the art of walking on snow bridges and also to the challenges of descending down the slippery mountain. Hampta pass is a well-rounded trek where you see different landscapes ranging from forest to meadows to snow and also get a glimpse of mountain risks like rough terrain and high altitude challenges. For all good reasons, Hampta Pass is one of the best treks in Himachal. Trekking duration is 5 days.


Deo Tibba Base Camp

Enjoy the mesmerizing view of Deo Tibba (6,001m), Indrasan Peak (6,220m) and Inderkila Peak (4,940m) along with the glaciers of Jagatsukh and mini Chandratal lake. Deo Tibba not only offers the great view of some famous peaks of Himachal Pradesh but also has diverse flora and fauna. Deo Tibba is flooded with herbs that have medicinal properties, known to cure fever, stomach ache, and even stinky feet. The moderate gradient and ease of accessibility make it a good trek for beginners as well as experienced persons and. No wonder it is one of the stunning treks in Himachal. Trekking duration is 6 days.


Buran Ghati

Buran Ghati is one of the most beautiful treks in Himachal Pradesh. This newly discovered trail is becoming widely popular due to its untouched beauty and amazing campsites. The campsites are picture perfect with huge mountains in the background and vast meadows bordered by pine trees. Buran Ghati is one such trek where you get to see every aspect of nature, ranging from meadows of Dayara Bugyal to streams and waterfalls at Chandranahan. The mountain pass from Dhunda and snow wall at Buran Pass are also the star performers along this trail. This picture perfect trek is a must do trek in Himachal. Trekking duration is 8 days.


Pin Parvati Pass

Situated at the height of 17,380 feet, Pin Parvati Pass trek is a true stunner, starting from Phulga village of Kullu, Kasol and ending at Mud village of Spiti. Categorized as difficult and challenging, Pin Parvati Pass trail is an exciting trail that holds surprises up its sleeves on nearly every turn you take. Witness the tree line disappearing into a grassland of wildflowers and disheveled shrubs, the uncanny Parvati valley through which the Parvati river chisels its curvy path and the equally contrasting deserted mountains bearing the spell of Spiti’s barrenness. And that's not all!! Traversing 78 KM on foot in 10 days, you don't only experience the transition of the landscape from green paradise to barren mountains, but also a transition between two cultures, from Hinduism to Buddhism, a remarkable rarity. This trek is one of the most challenging treks in Himachal also the most beautiful. Trekking duration is 12 days.


Friendship Peak

Craving a view of three mountain ranges? The Himalayas, Pir Panjal and the Dhauladhar Range of Himachal Pradesh can all be soaked in from Friendship Peak. Friendship peak is a high altitude trek, reaching 17,352 feet, yet does not require any technical knowledge or mountaineering experience. It does, however, demand good physical stamina. During these 8 days of the trek, you will experience a lot of snow and spectacular view of several peaks like Hanuman Tibba, Shitidhar, Indra Asan and Deo Tibba. Trekking duration is 8 days.
